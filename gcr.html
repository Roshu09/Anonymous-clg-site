WHY CHOOSE US?

Best Campus

The campus of the college spread in 5.63 acres. It is brimming with exuberance and enthusiasm while providing the students the necessary atmosphere and facilities for the promotion of innovations in education leading to re-structuring of courses, new methods of teaching, training, and learning. We have a big beautiful newly constructed Botanical garden just beside the college pond which attracts the visitors and students as well.

Best Teachers

The college has some of the talented and dedicated thought leaders who come from the prime institutions from the states. Not only are they academecaly and professionally amongst the best, but have high moral standards and ethical values, so that they can be true role models.

Best Library

Our library is well-stocked with latest books (both fiction and non-fiction) and research and industry journals. The College library is open from 10:00 A.M. to 4:00 P.M. on working days. We also organise book fair & exhibition in the college campus. The Exhibition is open for the student and staff. It exhibits various books and journals from various publishers.
